Motivation

Personally, I (Hiep) am intrigued by abstract art and geometric pattern. It is not necessarily as significant as Picasso's masterpieces with astounding colors and immersed deep meanings. Simply, a random pavement pattern constructed by thousands of carved tiles on the street or patterns generated by multiple circular waves caused by random rain drops on a random shallow pond leave me a deep fascinating impression. I admire the randomness and coincidence in life accidentally contribute to unexpectedly stunning masterpieces. Pursuing a Computer Science degree allows myself to use coding to create artworks with randomness - Generative Art.

Fortunately, I have a chance to enroll this course which will equip me with profound graphics knowledge. Furthermore, meeting Hoa is another fortune. She can help me to extend the collection of generative arts as well as implement an exhibition on a website with user interactions.

Objective

Our final accomplished project will be a website displaying a number of artworks. Perhaps, the website also allow user to interact with them such color picker or mouse dragging
